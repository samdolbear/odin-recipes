Lazy Potato Soup

The most scrumptious and lowest effort potato soup one could ever want. Way wait to enjoy a low effort, low calorie delight that is this wonderfully silky potato soup!

Ingredients

  • 500g potatoes, chopped roughly
  • Just enough water to cover
  • Large pot
  • One medium onion, about 100g, chopped roughly
  • Carrot, medium, about 100g, chopped roughly
  • Anything laying around in the fridge

Method

Do be sure to wash your veg and skin or chop off bad parts as necessary.

  1. Clean and chop your potatoes and other veg. Placing them into the pot when done.
  2. Add water until you cover the potatoes and vegetables.
  3. Simmer for 40 minutes. Test readiness by poking a potato with a knife. Or test eating one. Look for the knife not penetrating or fragments of potato that are uncooked in the centre.
  4. If not done yet leave it simmering and come back in 5-10 minutes!
